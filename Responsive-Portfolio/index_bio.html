About Me

Nicole Wobbe-Espinoza has been working as an Assistant for more than 20 years. Currently employed at the Women's Resource Center at the University of Utah as an Administrative Assistant, Scholarship Coordinator, System Administrator and resident geek. She recently completed a Web Design Tools Certificate and is currently working toward a Full Stack Developer Certificate and completion of a bachelor's degree in Geography with an emphasis in GIS.

On a personal note she volunteers as an area coordinator for Southwest Airedale Terrier Rescue, is an avid dog lover and shares her life with her partner, four-legged kids, brothers, and friends. She also enjoys painting, reading, volleyball, swimming, and being in the outdoors.
